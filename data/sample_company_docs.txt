Company Refund Policy
=====================

After 30 days, refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis.
To request a refund, email support@example.com with your order number.

Shipping Policy
===============

Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days.
Express shipping (2–3 days) is available for an additional $15.
Free shipping on orders over $50.
We ship to the US, Canada, and most European countries.

Product Warranty
================

All hardware products come with a 1-year limited warranty.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects but not accidental damage.
Contact warranty@example.com to file a warranty claim.

Privacy Policy
==============

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
We collect email addresses for order confirmation and shipping updates only.
You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@example.com.
Data is stored on encrypted servers in the United States.
